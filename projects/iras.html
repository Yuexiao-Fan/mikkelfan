IRAS Data Analysis

Comprehensive tax data analysis and visualization for government agency with advanced statistical modeling and policy impact assessment.

This project, developed by Mikkel Fan, involves comprehensive tax data analysis for the Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS), focusing on revenue trends, compliance patterns, and policy impact assessment using advanced analytics techniques.

Problem

Government agencies needed comprehensive analysis of tax revenue patterns, compliance trends, and policy effectiveness to optimize tax collection strategies and identify potential revenue leakage points across different taxpayer segments.

Approach

  • Analyzed multi-year tax datasets using Python and advanced statistical methods to identify revenue trends and patterns.
  • Developed compliance scoring models to assess taxpayer behavior and predict potential non-compliance risks.
  • Created interactive dashboards using Tableau for real-time monitoring of tax collection metrics and KPIs.
  • Implemented policy impact analysis to measure the effectiveness of tax policy changes and recommendations.

        'seasonality': seasonal_patterns,
        'compliance': compliance_score,
        'policy_impact': policy_impact
    }

def calculate_compliance_metrics(df):
    # Risk scoring based on historical patterns
    risk_factors = ['filing_delay', 'payment_delay', 'audit_history']
    compliance_score = df[risk_factors].apply(
        lambda x: 1 - (x.sum() / len(risk_factors)), axis=1
    )
    return compliance_score
