Data Analytics Blog

Personal blog sharing insights on data analytics, business intelligence, and data science best practices with practical tutorials and case studies.

This project, created by Mikkel Fan, is a comprehensive Data Analytics Blog featuring in-depth articles, tutorials, and case studies on data science, business intelligence, and analytics best practices.

Problem

The data analytics community lacked accessible, practical content that bridges the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world applications. Many resources were either too technical or too superficial, leaving practitioners without actionable insights.

Approach

  • Created comprehensive tutorials covering SQL, Python, Power BI, and advanced analytics techniques.
  • Developed case studies based on real business scenarios with step-by-step solutions and code examples.
  • Implemented interactive content with visualizations, code snippets, and downloadable resources.
  • Built community engagement through comments, social sharing, and newsletter subscriptions.

Results

  • 50+ articles published covering various data analytics topics and techniques.
  • 10K+ monthly readers with growing engagement and community interaction.
  • Featured content in industry publications and shared across professional networks.
  • Educational impact helping data professionals advance their skills and careers.

What I Learned

  • Importance of clear, accessible technical writing for diverse audiences.
  • Value of practical examples and real-world case studies in educational content.
  • Community building and engagement strategies for technical blogs.
  • Content marketing and SEO optimization for technical publications.
